First number shapes the conversation. Go high (if selling) or low (if buying) but stay reasonable. Extreme anchors backfire. Research market rates beforehand.

After making an offer, shut up. Let them fill the silence. Most people can't stand it and cave. Discomfort is a negotiation tool. Silence = power.
